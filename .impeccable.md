## Design Context

### Users
Sport venue operators, coaches, and marketplace admins manage schedules, pricing, policy controls, and risk settings. Sessions happen during active operations where quick scanning and confident decisions matter.

### Brand Personality
Competitive, trustworthy, and decisive. Interface should feel premium and energetic while preserving strong clarity for business-critical actions.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first command center look with deep navy anchors, vivid amber accents, and sport-energy highlights. Visual style should avoid generic dashboard templates, keep strong contrast, and make key controls feel high-importance.

### Design Principles
1. Emphasize hierarchy with assertive scale contrast.
2. Reserve bright accents for action and status moments.
3. Keep administrative controls instantly scannable.
4. Use motion and depth sparingly but intentionally.
5. Preserve accessibility and interaction reliability under all styling changes.
