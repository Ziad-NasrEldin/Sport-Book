# Feature List: SportBook Ultimate

## 1. Authentication & Access Control
- **User Registration**: Sign up via email/password or social login (future).
- **Login & Session Management**: Secure JWT-based authentication.
- **Password Recovery**: Reset password via email link.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Distinct permissions for Players, Coaches, Facility Managers, and Admins.
- **Account Verification**: Email verification for new users.

## 2. User Profile Module
- **Personal Details**: Manage name, contact info, and profile photo.
- **Preferences**: Set preferred language (Arabic/English) and favorite sports.
- **Wallet Summary**: View current balance and payment history.
- **Booking History**: Access past and upcoming sessions.
- **Favorites**: Save preferred facilities and coaches for quick access.

## 3. Facility Management Module
- **Facility Profile**: Manage facility name, description, and high-quality images.
- **Branch Management**: Create and manage multiple branches under one facility.
- **Court Creation**: Add courts with specific sport types and attributes.
- **Pricing Rules**: Define base prices and time-based pricing (peak vs. off-peak).
- **Operating Hours**: Set daily opening/closing times per branch.
- **Closure Rules**: Block slots for maintenance or private events.

## 4. Coach Management Module
- **Coach Profile**: Manage bio, specialization, and session duration.
- **Service Definition**: Define coaching services and pricing per session.
- **Availability Calendar**: Set working hours and recurring availability.
- **Session Rules**: Define cancellation and rescheduling policies.
- **Linked Facilities**: Associate with specific facilities or operate independently.

## 5. Discovery & Search Module
- **Smart Search**: Search by facility name, coach name, or sport type.
- **Geolocation**: Find nearby options using GPS and distance-aware sorting.
- **Filtering**: Filter by price, rating, availability, and branch.
- **Map View**: Interactive map showing nearby facilities and coaches.
- **Featured Listings**: Highlight top-rated or promoted facilities/coaches.

## 6. Booking Engine
- **Slot Reservation**: Real-time slot selection with conflict prevention.
- **Recurring Bookings**: Book weekly or monthly sessions in one flow.
- **Approval Workflow**: Support for both instant and manual approval-based bookings.
- **Booking Statuses**: Track lifecycle (Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled).
- **Conflict Detection**: Prevent double-booking of courts or coaches.

## 7. Cart & Checkout Module
- **Unified Cart**: Mix facility and coach bookings in a single transaction.
- **Coupon Engine**: Apply percentage or fixed-amount discounts.
- **Tax & Fee Handling**: Automated calculation of subtotals and taxes.
- **Payment Integration**: Support for multiple gateways (Stripe, Paymob, etc.).
- **Confirmation Flow**: Post-payment summary and digital receipt.

## 8. Notification Module
- **In-App Notifications**: Real-time alerts for booking updates and reminders.
- **WhatsApp Deep Linking**: Direct links for quick communication with operators.
- **Email Alerts**: Automated confirmations and cancellation notices.
- **Admin Alerts**: Notifications for new facility/coach registrations.

## 9. Reviews & Ratings Module
- **Post-Booking Reviews**: Only users with completed sessions can leave reviews.
- **Rating Breakdown**: Rate facilities and coaches on specific criteria.
- **Moderation Tools**: Admin tools to review and manage user feedback.
- **Abuse Prevention**: Logic to prevent fake or duplicate reviews.

## 10. Analytics & Reporting Module
- **Revenue Dashboard**: View monthly and yearly revenue trends.
- **Usage Analysis**: Identify peak hours and most booked sports.
- **Performance Snapshots**: Quick KPIs for facilities and coaches.
- **Export Support**: Download reports in CSV or PDF format.

## 11. Admin Control Module
- **User Moderation**: Manage and verify user accounts.
- **Platform Settings**: Configure global pricing, sports, and categories.
- **Coupon Management**: Create and track promotional campaigns.
- **Audit Logs**: Track all administrative actions for security.
- **Content Management**: Update platform-wide text and configurations.
